The Problem

ETHDenver is massive. Coordination is hard.

  • 🌊 100+ side events across Denver every week -- hackathons, talks, parties, summits, workshops. Nobody can track them all.
  • 👥 Fragmented coordination. Your crew is split across group chats, Luma invites, Google Sheets, and DMs. Who's going where?
  • 🚫 Discovery is broken. The best events aren't always the loudest. First-timers miss hidden gems. Veterans miss new builders.
  • 🤔 No shared context. You arrive in Denver and ask "what's happening tonight?" -- but there's no single answer.
The Solution

One place to discover, coordinate, and go together

FlowB aggregates every side event into one feed, lets you form crews, and rewards exploration. Your ETHDenver companion -- free and open.
