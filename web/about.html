About FlowB

Your companion for navigating ETHDenver. Discover events, form crews, earn points, and never miss the best side events.

Our Mission

ETHDenver is massive -- hundreds of side events, hackathons, parties, and meetups spread across Denver. It's easy to miss the best ones or lose track of your friends.

FlowB solves this. We aggregate every side event into one place, let you form crews with your friends, and reward you for exploring. Whether you're a first-timer or a Denver veteran, FlowB helps you make the most of the week.

Built by the community, for the community. Free and open.

Frequently Asked Questions

FlowB is your ETHDenver companion app. We aggregate 100+ side events, hackathons, parties, and meetups into one place so you never miss what matters. Form crews with friends, earn points, and explore Denver together.
RSVP to events, check in at venues with your crew, invite friends, complete daily quests, and engage with the community. Points unlock milestones and leaderboard rankings.
Crews are groups of friends exploring ETHDenver together. Create or join a crew to coordinate schedules, check in together at events, and earn bonus crew points. Crews compete on the leaderboard for bragging rights.
Yes, FlowB is completely free. No sign-up required to browse events. Create an account to RSVP, join crews, and earn points.
Set a reminder on any event and FlowB will notify you before it starts. Reminders are delivered via Telegram DM (primary) or Farcaster push notification (fallback). You can customize timing in your notification settings.
FlowB is available everywhere: on the web at flowb.me, as a Telegram mini app, as a Farcaster mini app in Warpcast, and soon as a native mobile app for iOS and Android. Your account syncs across all platforms.
FlowB is built by a small team of ETHDenver regulars who got tired of missing great events. Event data is powered by eGator. We're open source and community-driven.
