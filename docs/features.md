# FlowB Features

FlowB is your event companion for discovering events, coordinating with friends, and earning rewards.

## Events

Browse hundreds of events aggregated from multiple sources including Luma, Eventbrite, Lemonade, and community submissions.

- **Search & filter** by category, date, keyword, and city
- **RSVP** going or maybe to track your schedule
- **See who's going** from your flow (friends and crew)
- **Event reminders** before things start
- **Smart event links** that detect your platform (Telegram, Farcaster, WhatsApp, or web)
- **Community submit** — anyone can add an event link via bot or web
- **Event boost** — bid to feature your event at the top (USDC or Stripe)

## Flow (Friends)

Connect with friends to see each other's event plans.

- **Share your flow link** — send `flowb.me/f/{your-code}` to a friend
- **Instant connection** — when they tap it, you're both connected
- **See mutual plans** — know when friends are going to the same events
- **Mute or remove** connections anytime
- **Nearby friends** — see friends who are close by

## Crews

Create or join group flows for your squad, DAO, or project team.

- **Create a crew** with a custom name and emoji
- **Invite members** via crew link or personal tracked invite
- **Crew chat** — message your crew directly
- **Crew leaderboard** — see who's earning the most points
- **Crew meetups** — earn bonus points when multiple members check in together
- **Admin tools** — approve join requests, promote members, manage settings
- **Public discovery** — browse and request to join open crews
- **Where's my crew** — see real-time check-in locations of crew members

## AI Chat

Natural language AI assistant powered by tool-augmented chat.

- **Event search** — "find me DeFi events tonight"
- **Lead management** — "add lead Sarah at Acme"
- **Meeting scheduling** — "schedule coffee with Mike tomorrow"
- **Agent memory** — remembers context across conversations
- **Code intelligence** — engineering briefs, velocity reports (CuFlow)

## Business Tools

CRM and productivity features built into the chat.

- **Leads** — add, update, and track leads through pipeline stages
- **Pipeline view** — visual funnel of your deal stages
- **Meetings** — schedule, share links, get briefings, send follow-ups
- **Automations** — trigger-action rules (auto-follow-up, lead nurture, reminders)
- **Referral programs** — create programs, track commissions, manage payouts
- **Earnings** — view referral commissions and payout history
- **Kanban board** — visual task/lead management at kanban.flowb.me

## AI Agents

Personal AI agent slots with skill marketplace.

- **10 agent slots** per user
- **Equip skills** — customize what your agent can do
- **USDC micropayments** — agents can transact on your behalf
- **Event boosting** — agents can bid in boost auctions

## Points & Rewards

Every action earns points toward milestone levels.

- **6 milestone levels** from Explorer to Legend
- **Streak bonuses** for consecutive daily activity
- **Tiered invite rewards** for growing your crew
- **Daily caps** to keep things fair
- **Global crew ranking** based on combined member points

See the full [Points System](/points) for details.

## Notifications

Stay in the loop across platforms.

- **Telegram DMs** as the primary notification channel
- **WhatsApp messages** for WhatsApp users
- **Signal messages** for Signal users
- **Farcaster push** for Farcaster mini app users
- **Expo push** for mobile app users
- **Email digests** — weekly summary of activity
- **Event reminders** at your chosen intervals
- **Flow activity** — get notified when friends RSVP or check in
- **Crew updates** — join requests, new members, meetup alerts
- **Keyword alerts** — set topics like "AI", "party", or "DeFi" and get notified when matching events are discovered. Route alerts to your DM or a crew chat
- **Context notifications** — smart push based on location and time
- **Meeting reminders** — before scheduled meetings

## Social Posting (SocialB)

Cross-platform content distribution.

- **Auto-repost** — Farcaster casts automatically adapted and posted to other platforms
- **Multi-platform** — connect and post to multiple social accounts
- **Organization support** — manage social for your crew or project

## Support

- **Email support** — send to support@flowb.me, AI drafts replies, admin responds via TG
- **Bug reports** — `/reportbug` command in bot
- **Feature suggestions** — `/suggestfeature` command in bot
