## 5. Break-Even Analysis

### Fixed Costs (Annual)

| Cost Category | Range |
|---------------|-------|
| Website/hosting | $500 |
| Open Collective fees | 5% of funds |
| Insurance | $2,000-5,000 |
| Legal (cooperative maintenance) | $1,000-3,000 |
| Tool/equipment maintenance | $1,000-3,000 |
| Marketing/community | $2,000-5,000 |
| **Total Fixed** | **$6,500-16,500/year** |

### Variable Costs (per kit)

| Cost Component | Percentage of Kit Price |
|----------------|------------------------|
| Materials (at group buy pricing) | 70-80% |
| Packaging/shipping | 5-10% |
| Quality control/testing | 3-5% |
| **Total Variable** | **78-95%** |

### Break-Even Point

- Average kit margin: 15-25% after materials and shipping.
- At 20% average margin:
  - Need $33K-83K in annual sales to cover fixed costs.
  - That equals 30-80 small kits or 5-10 complete kits per year.
  - Achievable in Year 1 with an active community.

### Break-Even Sensitivity

| Scenario | Avg Margin | Annual Sales Needed | Kit Equivalent |
|----------|-----------|---------------------|----------------|
| Conservative (high costs) | 15% | $110K | ~10 complete Tier 1 kits |
| Base case | 20% | $83K | ~7 complete Tier 1 kits |
| Optimistic (group buy savings) | 25% | $66K | ~6 complete Tier 1 kits |

## Summary

The FlowMobile Sovereign Quad occupies a unique market position: no commercial vehicle at any price offers the complete sovereignty stack (V2H power export, solar charging, mesh communications, water purification, EMP hardening, digital sovereignty, agriculture tools, and full right to repair). At $25-35K for the Expeditionary tier, it undercuts comparable military platforms by 50%+ while offering capabilities that simply do not exist in the consumer market.

The financial model is built on cooperative principles with multiple revenue streams (kits, workshops, consulting, carbon credits) that reduce dependency on any single funding source. Break-even is achievable in Year 1 with modest kit sales, and the project reaches self-sustainability by Year 3 with projected revenue of $385K-635K.

The funding strategy layers grants (federal/state/private), crowdfunding (Kickstarter + Open Collective), and cooperative equity to build capital while preserving the open-source commitment and community ownership structure that make the project worth building in the first place.
